CLASS RENAMING INSTRUCTIONS
============================

CURRENT FORMAT: AI - Year 2 Sem 3, AI - Year 2 Sem 4, etc.
NEW FORMAT: I YR CSE-A, I YR AIML-B, II YR ECE-A, etc.

STEPS TO UPDATE:
1. Run the SQL script: rename-classes-to-new-format.sql in Supabase SQL Editor
2. This will rename all existing classes to the new format
3. The admin UI has been updated to show only the class name (no separate department column)

NAMING CONVENTION:
- I YR = First Year (previously Year 2, Sem 3-4)
- II YR = Second Year (previously Year 3, Sem 5-6)  
- III YR = Third Year (previously Year 4, Sem 7-8)
- IV YR = Fourth Year (if applicable)

EXAMPLES:
- I YR CSE-A (Computer Science, Section A)
- I YR AIML-B (AI & ML, Section B)
