Design Guidelines

Logo Specifications

  • Concept: User icon with checkmark represents verified attendance
  • Colors: Gradient from emerald-500 to emerald-300
  • Typography: Black weight (900), tight tracking
  • Minimum Size: 32x32px for icon, 120px width for full logo
  • Clear Space: Maintain padding equal to 25% of logo height
  • Background: Works best on dark backgrounds (#000, #111827)
